## Capture tips

- Combat clips are most impactful at 30–60 fps, but a high-fps GIF will bloat fast — convert to MP4 for anything > 4 seconds.
- The anim-notify lifecycle is visually clear when you slow-mo a combo by ~50%; consider rendering a slowed clip for the notify-driven section.
- For the enemy director clip, capture from a high-angle camera so the flanking/encirclement behavior reads clearly.
